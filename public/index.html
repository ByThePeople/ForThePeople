About Us

  • Sara Jade

    Sara is a pharmacist with who is changing careers. She has made the decision to go back to the future, doing what she almost did after graduating high school - software development! Along the way, she has obtained a degree in Information Systems, Software Development Track, from Bellevue College. She has also taught herself via Codecdademy, the Railsbridge group, the Flatiron School, and Ada Developers Academy's Jumpstart program. Now she is an apprentice at Microsoft via Apprenti training at Codefellows.

  • Britini Malloy

    After getting out of the Navy I worked at a bank and was looking for ideas before deciding to go back to school to use my GI Bill Benefits. I could not stand the system we used to do our daily work. I was constantly coming up with new ideas that would make the system better and more user friendly, and make our work more efficient. I just had no idea how to do it, much less how to go about doing any such things. So, I enrolled in school in a Computer Information Systems program and I worked for both my Bachelor's then my Master's. Unfortunately, it wasn't enough to help me get a job. Then the Apprenti program came along and gave me this great opportunity. I have been striving ever since to learn and be worthy of my opportunity!
